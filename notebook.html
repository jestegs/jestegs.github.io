Notes

There may be something living under the stones behind my house.

Every night the foxes come and sniff around them...

My sister thinks that there is something dead under there, and that they keep coming back to eat it.

But I've watched them do this for days.

I like to think that maybe they have a home down there, and that they are just a little shy

Because otherwise the thing that lives there would know who I was

And know each time I'm coming.
